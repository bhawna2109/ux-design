Hey there! I'm Bhawna (pronounces name bhaav-naa)

Nice to meet you! I'm a Product Designer with 6 years of formal design education and 8+ years of industry experience. I specialize in applying product thinking to break down complex problems and craft helpful experiences.

I also have a deep interest in learning about the underlying technologies that fuel the designs I create. During my undergrad and grad school, I took courses to dip my toes in coding (Python, writing accessible front-end code), and theoretical computer science. With a desire to build something tangible, I designed and self-coded this website from scratch, and had a lot of fun doing so.

Here is a link to my resume. I'm based in the Bay Area but originally hail from India. Outside of work, I enjoy activities that involve movement and being close to nature – climbing, exercising, yoga, skiing, hiking, camping, and going for walks.
